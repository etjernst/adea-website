Hello and welcome to the official home of the Australasian Development Economics Association. We are excited to launch this platform where we will share our news, events, research, insights and opinions on all things related to development economics in the Australasian region.

### Who we are

We are a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and advance the field of development economics in the Australasian region. We represent a community of researchers who share a common passion for understanding and improving the lives of people in developing countries.

### Why we exist

We believe that development economics is a vital and relevant discipline that can contribute to solving some of the most pressing and complex challenges of our time, around questions of poverty, inequality, health, education, environment, gender, migration, trade and conflict.

However, we also recognise that development economists face some challenges in our region, such as limited funding, limited recognition, and limited opportunities for collaboration with government and non-governmental organisations working in the region. We want to change that by creating a network that supports and connects development economists with each other and with development practitioners and funders in the region and beyond.

### What we do

We are developing a range of initiatives that aim to achieve our mission. These include building a strong and vibrant community of development economists in the region; improving the quality and impact of research on development issues; increasing the recognition of development economics as a priority area; enhancing the capacity and skills of development economists; and raising the awareness and appreciation of development economics among the public, media, educators and students.

### How you can join us

If you are interested in joining us, we would love to hear from you. We welcome active researchers from any region of the world who share our vision and mission. You can also subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on our latest news and events.

We look forward to welcoming you to our association and working together to create a better world through development economics. Thank you for your interest and support!
