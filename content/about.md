intro: We are the Australasian Development Economics Association, a non-profit organisation that aims to promote and advance the field of development economics in the Australasian region. We are a community of researchers who share a common passion for understanding and improving the lives of people in developing countries.
mission_heading: What we set out to do
mission:
  - Build a strong and vibrant community of development economists in the Australasian region, fostering collaboration, exchange of ideas, mentoring and networking opportunities.
  - Improve the quality and impact of research on development issues, supporting innovative and policy-relevant projects, disseminating findings to a wide audience, and engaging with key stakeholders and decision-makers.
  - Increase the funding and recognition of development economics as a priority area, advocating for more resources and support from funding bodies, academic institutions, government agencies and NGOs.
  - Enhance the capacity and skills of development economists in the region, providing training, workshops, seminars, conferences and other learning opportunities.
  - Raise the awareness and appreciation of development economics among the public, media, educators and students, highlighting its relevance, importance and contributions to society.
